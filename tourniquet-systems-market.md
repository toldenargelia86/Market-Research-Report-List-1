<p><p>The Tourniquet Systems market is primarily categorized into two types: Tourniquet Instruments and Tourniquet Cuffs. Tourniquet Instruments encompass devices employed to apply pressure and control blood flow during surgical procedures or emergencies. These include manual and pneumatic tourniquets, designed for precision and ease of use. Tourniquet Cuffs, on the other hand, consist of inflatable sleeves that encircle limbs, effectively restricting blood circulation to facilitate surgical access or manage trauma. Both types are crucial in enhancing patient safety and surgical outcomes.</p></p>

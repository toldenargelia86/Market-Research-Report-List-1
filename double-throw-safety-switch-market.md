<p><strong>The Double Throw Safety Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-fusible Disconnect</li><li>Fusible Disconnect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Throw Safety Switch market is divided into two main types: Non-fusible Disconnect and Fusible Disconnect. Non-fusible Disconnect switches allow the safe isolation of electrical circuits without fuses, providing flexibility and ease of maintenance. They are commonly used in applications where overcurrent protection is managed externally. In contrast, Fusible Disconnect switches incorporate fuses for overcurrent protection, offering added safety by interrupting power in case of faults. Both types serve critical roles in industrial and commercial electrical systems.</p></p>

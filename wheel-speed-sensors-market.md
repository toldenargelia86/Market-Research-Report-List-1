<p><ul><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li><li>Hall Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wheel speed sensors are crucial components in modern vehicles, providing real-time data for systems like anti-lock braking and stability control. Two main types dominate the market: Magnetic Electric and Hall Effect sensors. Magnetic Electric sensors utilize magnetic fields to measure wheel rotation, offering reliability in harsh conditions. In contrast, Hall Effect sensors employ semiconductor technology to detect wheel speed through voltage changes, providing accuracy and responsiveness. Both types play vital roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>

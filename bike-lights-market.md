<p><ul><li>Headlight</li><li>Taillight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bike lights market is primarily divided into two segments: headlights and taillights. Headlights are designed to illuminate the path ahead, enhancing visibility for riders during low-light conditions and improving safety. They come in various brightness levels and mounting options. Taillights, on the other hand, focus on increasing the visibility of cyclists to others, particularly motorists from behind. These lights often feature blinking modes and can be mounted on bike frames or helmets, ensuring cyclists are seen while riding.</p></p>

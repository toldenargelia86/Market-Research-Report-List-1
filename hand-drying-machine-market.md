<p><strong>The Hand Drying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand-in</li><li>Hand-under</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand drying machine market is categorized into two main types: hand-in and hand-under machines. Hand-in dryers require users to insert their hands into a designated slot, where warm air is directed to dry them quickly. In contrast, hand-under dryers operate by allowing users to place their hands beneath the unit, with air blown upwards for drying. Both types emphasize efficiency and hygiene, catering to various preferences and settings in public restrooms, workplaces, and commercial spaces.</p></p>

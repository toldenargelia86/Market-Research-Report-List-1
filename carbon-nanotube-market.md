<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carbon Nanotube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT)</li><li>Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT)</li><li>Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon nanotube market is primarily segmented into three types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT), and Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT). SWNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, offering unique electrical and thermal conductivity. MWNTs contain multiple concentric cylinders, enhancing mechanical strength and stability. DWNTs, featuring two walls, provide a compromise between the attributes of SWNTs and MWNTs. Each type has distinct applications in electronics, composites, and nanotechnology, driving market growth.</p></p>

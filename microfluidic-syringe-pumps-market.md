<p><p>The microfluidic syringe pumps market consists of two main types: single-channel and two-channel. Single-channel pumps are designed to deliver precise fluid volumes through one channel, making them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy in low-flow conditions. In contrast, two-channel pumps can independently control two fluid streams simultaneously, allowing for more complex experiments and applications, such as mixing or sequential delivery of different fluids. Both types cater to various research and clinical needs in fields like biotechnology and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

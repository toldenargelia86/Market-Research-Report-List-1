<p><p>The powder-based needle-free injector market comprises various types: Jet, Spring, Laser, and Vibration. Jet injectors utilize high-pressure liquid jets to deliver medication through the skin without needles. Spring injectors rely on mechanical springs to propel the drug into the tissue. Laser injectors use focused laser energy to create microchannels in the skin for drug delivery. Vibration injectors employ high-frequency vibrations to facilitate the absorption of powdered medication through the skin, ensuring a painless and efficient delivery method.</p></p>

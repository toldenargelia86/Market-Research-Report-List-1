<p><p>The material handling tire market primarily consists of two types: pneumatic tires and cushion tires. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide superior shock absorption and traction, making them ideal for rough outdoor surfaces and uneven terrains. Conversely, cushion tires are solid rubber with no air, offering enhanced durability and stability on smooth indoor surfaces. Each type caters to specific operational needs, with pneumatic tires favoring versatility and cushion tires providing reliability in warehouse and distribution settings.</p></p>

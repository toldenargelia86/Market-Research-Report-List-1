<p><p>The multi-channel electronic circuit breaker market encompasses three primary types: low voltage circuit breakers, medium voltage circuit breakers, and high voltage circuit breakers. Low voltage circuit breakers are designed for applications under 1,000 volts, protecting circuits in residential and commercial settings. Medium voltage circuit breakers operate between 1 kV and 36 kV, commonly used in industrial and utility applications. High voltage circuit breakers function above 36 kV, safeguarding transmission systems and large industrial facilities from electrical faults and overloads. Each type caters to specific voltage requirements and protection needs.</p></p>

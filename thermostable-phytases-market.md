<p><p>The thermostable phytases market comprises primarily two types: acid phytase and alkaline phytase. Acid phytases function optimally in low pH environments, making them suitable for applications in animal feed, especially in monogastric species, where they enhance phosphorus availability during digestion. In contrast, alkaline phytases thrive in higher pH conditions, benefiting ruminant animals and certain industrial applications. Both types help reduce environmental impact by improving phosphorus utilization and decreasing waste, contributing to more sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>

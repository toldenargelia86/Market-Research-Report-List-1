<p><p>Cell culture microplates are essential tools in biological research and pharmaceutical industries. The market for these plates is categorized into three types: flat-bottom, U-bottom, and V-bottom. Flat-bottom plates provide a large surface area, ideal for cell attachment and growth. U-bottom plates enhance sedimentation and enable better mixing, suitable for suspension cultures. V-bottom plates facilitate the collection of cells or supernatants, making them effective for assay applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on experimental needs.</p></p>

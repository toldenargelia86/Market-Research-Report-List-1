<p><p>The loft insulation material market includes various types such as EPS/XPS, RockWool, PIR/PU, GlassWool, and others. EPS/XPS (Expanded/Extruded Polystyrene) offers lightweight, moisture-resistant insulation. RockWool, made from volcanic rock, provides fire resistance and soundproofing. PIR/PU (Polyisocyanurate/Polyurethane) delivers high thermal efficiency with thin layers. GlassWool, crafted from recycled glass, is cost-effective and offers excellent thermal and acoustic insulation. Other materials include natural fibers and aerogels, catering to niche needs. Each type varies in thermal performance, cost, and application suitability, addressing diverse insulation requirements in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>

<p><p>Cerebral vascular stents are specialized medical devices designed to support and maintain the patency of blood vessels in the brain, particularly in the treatment of conditions such as aneurysms and stenosis. <p><p>The cerebral vascular stent market is categorized into three main types: tantalum, stainless steel, and nitinol. Stainless steel stents are favored for their strength and resistance to corrosion, providing durability in vascular applications. Nitinol, a nickel-titanium alloy, is known for its unique elasticity and shape memory properties, allowing for optimal conformability and flexibility in various vascular anatomies. Each material serves distinct clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>Ultra-High Efficiency Toilets (UHETs) are designed to reduce water usage while maintaining performance. The market is divided into two main types: single-flush and dual-flush toilets. Single-flush types utilize a consistent flush volume for all waste, typically using 1.28 gallons per flush or less. In contrast, dual-flush models offer two flushing options, allowing users to choose a lower volume for liquid waste and a higher volume for solid waste, promoting further water conservation. Both types contribute to sustainable bathroom practices.</p></p>

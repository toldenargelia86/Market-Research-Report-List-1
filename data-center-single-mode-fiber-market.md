"Standard Single Mode Fiber" is designed for long-distance data transmission, utilizing a thin core that allows light to travel directly down the fiber, minimizing modal dispersion. In contrast, "Specially Designed Single-mode Optical Fiber" includes variations tailored for specific applications, such as increased bandwidth or enhanced performance in harsh environments. These specialized fibers address unique networking needs, making them essential for advanced data center infrastructure and high-capacity networking solutions.</p></p>

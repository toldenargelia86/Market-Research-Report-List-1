<p><p>The Non-Isolated DC-DC Converter market can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I converters include Buck and Boost converters, focusing on voltage step-down or step-up functionalities without output isolation. These are widely used for efficient power management in devices requiring voltage adjustments. Type II includes topologies like Buck-Boost and Cuk converters, which allow for more versatile output voltage configurations, enhancing their applicability in various electronic systems, especially where dual voltage levels are essential.</p></p>

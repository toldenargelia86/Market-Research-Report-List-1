<p><p>The infrared (IR) sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Infrared (TIR) sensors and Quantum Infrared (QIR) sensors. TIR sensors detect emitted thermal radiation, typically from objects at room temperature, making them ideal for applications in temperature measurement and surveillance. In contrast, QIR sensors operate based on quantum effects to detect infrared light, providing higher sensitivity and faster response times. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, healthcare, and security, enhancing functionality and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>

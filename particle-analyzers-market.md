<p><ul><li>Laser Particle Analyzer</li><li>Image Particle Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The particle analyzers market primarily includes Laser Particle Analyzers and Image Particle Analyzers. Laser Particle Analyzers utilize laser diffraction techniques to measure particle size and distribution in various samples, providing rapid and precise results suitable for both liquids and powders. In contrast, Image Particle Analyzers employ advanced imaging technology, capturing and analyzing images of particles to provide detailed morphological data. Both types are essential in industries such as pharmaceuticals, materials science, and environmental monitoring for quality control and research applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Nuclear steam generators (SG) are critical components in nuclear power plants, tasked with converting heat from nuclear fission into steam for electricity generation. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Once Through Steam Generators (OTSG) and Recirculating Steam Generators. OTSGs provide a single pass of water through the heat exchanger, producing steam for turbines, while Recirculating Steam Generators retain water in the system, enhancing heat exchange efficiency through multiple passes, optimizing overall steam generation and plant performance.</p></p>

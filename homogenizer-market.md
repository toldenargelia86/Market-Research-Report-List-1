<p><p>The homogenizer market is categorized into several types based on technology and application. Rotary homogenizers use high-speed mechanical forces to blend materials, ideal for low-viscosity substances. Ultrasonic homogenizers utilize high-frequency sound waves to create intense shear forces, suitable for dispersing nanoparticles and emulsifying complex mixtures. 3D homogenizers combine rotational and axial flows for enhanced mixing efficiency in various formulations. Other types may include pressure homogenizers and batch homogenizers, catering to diverse industrial needs across pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics sectors.</p></p>

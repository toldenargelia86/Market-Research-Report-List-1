<p><p>Boxed gloves for the food market include nitrile, latex, and vinyl gloves, each serving distinct purposes. Nitrile gloves offer superior puncture resistance and are latex-free, making them ideal for those with allergies. Latex gloves provide excellent dexterity and fit, suited for tasks requiring tactile sensitivity, though they may cause allergic reactions. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks, though less durable than nitrile and latex. Together, these gloves cater to various hygiene and safety needs in food handling environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The strain sensors market is primarily divided into two types: ordinary strain sensors and reusable strain sensors. Ordinary strain sensors, often used in fixed applications, measure deformation in materials under stress and typically have a single-use design. In contrast, reusable strain sensors are designed for multiple applications, allowing for cost-effective measurement by being relocated and used again in different projects. This versatility makes reusable sensors particularly attractive in industries where frequent monitoring and adjustments are necessary.</p></p>

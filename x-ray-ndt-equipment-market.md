<p><ul><li>CR</li><li>DR</li><li>CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray NDT equipment market comprises three main types: Conventional Radiography (CR), Digital Radiography (DR), and Computed Tomography (CT). CR uses film to capture images, relying on traditional methods for image processing. DR eliminates film, offering immediate digital images for real-time analysis, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. CT provides 3D imaging, enabling detailed internal assessments of complex structures. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, facilitating effective inspection and quality control processes.</p></p>

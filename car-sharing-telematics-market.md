<p><p>The Car Sharing Telematics Market includes three main types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated systems. **Embedded** systems have telematics hardware built directly into the vehicle, offering seamless connectivity and functionality. **Tethered** systems rely on external devices, such as smartphones, to provide telematics services, making them cost-effective but dependent on external hardware. **Integrated** systems combine embedded hardware with smartphone connectivity, offering a hybrid solution that balances performance and flexibility. Each type caters to different needs in car-sharing services, balancing cost, convenience, and functionality.</p></p>

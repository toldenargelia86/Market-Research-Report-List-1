<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ink and toner market is primarily divided into two categories: liquid and powder. Liquid ink is typically used in inkjet printers and is composed of dye or pigment suspended in a liquid carrier. It is known for its vibrant colors and smooth application. In contrast, powder toner is used in laser printers and consists of finely ground plastic particles and pigment. Toner is fuser to paper through heat, resulting in crisp text and durable images. Each type serves distinct printing needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The Light Guide Plate (LGP) market features various types based on design and manufacturing techniques. Edge LGP directs light from the edges, while Bottom LGP integrates light sources beneath the panel. Printed LGP incorporates patterns for enhanced light diffusion, contrasting with Non-printed LGP that maintains a uniform surface. Injection Molding LGP utilizes molds for precise shapes and thickness, whereas Cut Forming LGP involves cutting sheets to desired sizes. Each type serves specific applications, catering to diverse lighting needs in displays and signage.</p></p>

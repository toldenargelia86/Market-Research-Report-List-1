<p><p>Clinical Practice Management Software (CPMS) is categorized into two primary types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise software is installed locally on a healthcare provider’s servers, offering control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based CPMS is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and reduced IT costs. This model typically operates on a subscription basis, making it more appealing for smaller practices seeking flexibility and lower upfront investment.</p></p>

<p><p>The Flame Retardant PPA (polyphthalamide) market is divided into two main types: halogen-based and halogen-free. Halogen type flame retardants incorporate elements like chlorine or bromine, offering effective fire resistance but raising environmental and health concerns. In contrast, halogen-free flame retardants utilize alternative chemistries, such as phosphorous-based compounds, providing a safer option for applications where environmental impact is critical. Both types serve various industries, including electronics, automotive, and textiles, to enhance safety and compliance standards.</p></p>

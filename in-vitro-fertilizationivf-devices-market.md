ICSI involves injecting a single sperm directly into an egg to enhance fertilization rates. FER allows for the use of previously frozen embryos, increasing flexibility in treatment. PGD enables genetic screening of embryos before implantation, ensuring the selection of healthy embryos, thereby improving overall success rates in IVF procedures.</p></p>

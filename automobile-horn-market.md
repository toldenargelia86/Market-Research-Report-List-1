<p><p>The automobile horn market includes two main types: air horns and electric horns. Air horns use compressed air to produce a loud, deep sound, commonly found in large vehicles like trucks and buses. Electric horns, powered by an electromagnetic system, are more compact and widely used in passenger cars due to their reliability and ease of installation. Both types serve the purpose of alerting others on the road, with air horns offering higher decibel levels and electric horns providing consistent performance.</p></p>

<p><p>The Air Source Heat Pump market comprises two primary types: Air-to-Air Heat Pumps and Air-to-Water Heat Pumps. Air-to-Air Heat Pumps transfer heat from the outside air to indoor spaces, providing efficient heating and cooling for residential and commercial buildings. Air-to-Water Heat Pumps, on the other hand, extract heat from the air to heat water for space heating, domestic hot water, or swimming pools. Both types promote energy efficiency and sustainability in heating solutions, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>

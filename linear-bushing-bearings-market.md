<p><p>Linear bushing bearings are categorized into three main types based on their pressure and application suitability. Standard type bearings are designed for general-purpose use, offering smooth linear motion under typical loads. Medium pressure type bearings provide enhanced performance and durability for applications with moderate loads, making them suitable for a variety of machinery. High pressure type bearings are built to handle significant loads and extreme conditions, ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications requiring maximum reliability and longevity.</p></p>

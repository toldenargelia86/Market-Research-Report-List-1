<p><p>SONAR systems and technology encompass various market types designed for different underwater applications. Multi-beam SONAR captures high-resolution images of the seafloor using multiple beams, ideal for detailed mapping. Diver Detection SONAR identifies and tracks divers for security purposes. Single Beam Scanning SONAR provides depth measurements along a straight line, useful for basic surveys. Synthetic Aperture SONAR enhances image quality over large areas by simulating a larger aperture. Side Scan SONAR produces detailed images of the seafloor, aiding in exploration and archaeological research.</p></p>

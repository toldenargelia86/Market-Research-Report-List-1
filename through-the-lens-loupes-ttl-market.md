<p><p>Through-The-Lens (TTL) loupes are dental or surgical magnifying devices that enhance visibility during procedures. They come in two primary types: Galilean lenses, which are lightweight and offer a wider field of view with lower magnification, making them suitable for general applications; and prismatic lenses, which provide higher magnification and a narrower field of view, ideal for detailed work. Both types are designed to improve precision and comfort while reducing physical strain on the practitioner.</p></p>

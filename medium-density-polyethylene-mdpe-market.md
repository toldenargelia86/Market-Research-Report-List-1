<p><p>Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) is a type of polyethylene characterized by its moderate density and strength, making it suitable for various applications, including pipes, packaging, and containers. The MDPE market can be segmented into two primary processes: the autoclave process and the tubular process. The autoclave process produces MDPE using high pressure and temperature in a reactor, yielding a versatile product. In contrast, the tubular process employs a continuous extrusion method, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness in producing MDPE products.</p></p>

<p><strong>The PPLN Waveguide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton in-diffused (RPE) Waveguides</li><li>Ridge-Waveguides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PPLN waveguide market primarily includes two types: proton in-diffused (RPE) waveguides and ridge waveguides. RPE waveguides utilize proton diffusion to achieve non-linear optical properties, enhancing conversion efficiency in applications like frequency doubling. Ridge waveguides, on the other hand, feature a defined ridge structure that confines light, allowing for improved integration and performance in compact devices. Both types cater to the growing demand for photonic devices, emphasizing high efficiency and miniaturization in telecommunications and sensing applications.</p></p>

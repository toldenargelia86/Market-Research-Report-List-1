<p><ul><li>Ball bearing</li><li>Roller bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axial roller bearing market is segmented into two main types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings utilize spherical balls to reduce friction between moving parts, providing high speed and low noise levels. Conversely, roller bearings employ cylindrical rollers, which offer greater load-carrying capacity and stability under heavy loads. Each type serves specific applications; ball bearings are ideal for lighter, faster machinery, while roller bearings excel in high-load environments, making both essential in various industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>

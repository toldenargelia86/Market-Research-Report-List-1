<p><p>The spinal imaging market comprises several key modalities: MRI, CT, X-ray, and ultrasound. MRI provides detailed images of soft tissues, making it ideal for evaluating spinal cord and nerve root conditions. CT scans offer cross-sectional views, useful for assessing complex fractures and diagnosing tumors. X-ray is commonly used for initial evaluations of spinal alignment and structural issues. Ultrasound, while less conventional for spinal imaging, can assist in guiding minimally invasive procedures and evaluating soft tissue abnormalities near the spine.</p></p>

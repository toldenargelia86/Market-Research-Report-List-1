<p><p>The ArF resist market comprises two main types: ArF dry resist and ArF immersion resist. ArF dry resist is utilized in photolithography processes for producing semiconductor devices, where it functions effectively under standard atmospheric conditions. In contrast, ArF immersion resist operates in an immersion lithography setup that uses a liquid medium to enhance resolution and pattern fidelity. Both types are essential for advancing semiconductor manufacturing technology, providing high resolution and performance for increasingly complex circuits.</p></p>

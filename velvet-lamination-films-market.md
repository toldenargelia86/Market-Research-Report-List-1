<p><p>The Velvet Lamination Films market consists of two primary types: Thermal Velvet Films and Wet Velvet Films. Thermal Velvet Films are applied using heat and pressure to create a soft, textured finish, ideal for high-quality packaging and promotional materials. In contrast, Wet Velvet Films utilize an adhesive application process, allowing for more flexibility in design and surface texture. Both types enhance the aesthetic appeal and tactile experience of products while providing protection against wear and tear.</p></p>

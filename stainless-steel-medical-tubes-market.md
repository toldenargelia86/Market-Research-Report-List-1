<p><p>The stainless steel medical tubes market is primarily categorized into three types: 304, 316, and others. Type 304 is widely used for its corrosion resistance and formability, making it suitable for various medical applications. Type 316 offers enhanced corrosion resistance, particularly against chlorides, making it ideal for surgical instruments and implants. The "others" category includes specialized alloys and grades tailored for specific medical requirements, such as higher strength or biocompatibility, catering to diverse healthcare applications.</p></p>

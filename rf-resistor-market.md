<p><p>The RF resistor market comprises several types, each serving specific functions. Feedback RF resistors are used in amplifier circuits to provide stability and linearity. Low capacitance RF resistors minimize signal distortion at high frequencies. Flanged RF resistors feature a mounting flange for secure installation, while flangeless RF resistors offer a compact design for space-constrained applications. Legacy resistors refer to older technologies still in use, often found in established systems, requiring compatibility with existing setups. Each type addresses unique performance and installation needs.</p></p>

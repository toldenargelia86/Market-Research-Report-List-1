<p><ul><li>Magnetic Peripheral Pump</li><li>Centrifugal Peripheral Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Pumps market is primarily divided into two types: Magnetic Peripheral Pumps and Centrifugal Peripheral Pumps. Magnetic Peripheral Pumps utilize magnetic forces to create flow, offering a seal-less design that reduces leakage and enhances efficiency. These pumps are ideal for applications requiring minimal maintenance and contamination-free operations. On the other hand, Centrifugal Peripheral Pumps use a rotating impeller to impart kinetic energy to the fluid, producing a high flow rate and pressure, making them suitable for various industrial and domestic applications.</p></p>

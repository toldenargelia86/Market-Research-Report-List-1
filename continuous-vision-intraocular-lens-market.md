<p><strong>The Continuous Vision Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Vision Intraocular Lens (IOL) market includes two primary types: hydrophobic acrylic IOLs and hydrophilic acrylic IOLs. Hydrophobic acrylic IOLs are designed to repel water, offering a reduced risk of complications and enhanced stability within the eye, making them ideal for patients with active lifestyles. In contrast, hydrophilic acrylic IOLs absorb water, providing excellent optical clarity and comfort but may be more prone to swelling. Both types aim to improve vision quality for cataract patients, enhancing their overall visual experience.</p></p>

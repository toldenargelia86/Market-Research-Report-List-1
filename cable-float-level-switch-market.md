<p><p>Cable float level switches are crucial for liquid level measurement and control in various industries. The top-mounted variety is installed vertically and detects liquid levels from above, making it suitable for tanks with limited space. In contrast, side-mounted switches are affixed to the tank's side and can sense level changes horizontally, ideal for larger tanks with skewed profiles. Both types offer reliable performance, but their installation choice depends on specific space constraints and application requirements.</p></p>

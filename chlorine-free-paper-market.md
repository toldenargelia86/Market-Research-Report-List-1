<p><ul><li>Totally Chlorine-free Paper</li><li>Elemental Chlorine-free Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chlorine-free paper market includes two main types: Totally Chlorine-Free (TCF) paper and Elemental Chlorine-Free (ECF) paper. TCF paper is produced without the use of chlorine or chlorine compounds in any part of the bleaching process, making it the more environmentally friendly option. In contrast, ECF paper uses chlorine dioxide instead of elemental chlorine, reducing harmful emissions while still utilizing some chlorine in the bleaching process. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly paper products.</p></p>

<p><p>The Cable & Accessories market comprises three main types based on voltage levels: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage cables operate at up to 1 kV, commonly used in residential and commercial applications for power distribution. Medium voltage cables, ranging from 1 kV to 33 kV, are utilized in industrial and utility sectors for substation and distribution networks. High voltage cables, exceeding 33 kV, are essential for long-distance transmission and connecting power plants to grids, enabling efficient energy distribution.</p></p>

<p><p>The refrigerative dehumidifier market includes two primary types: tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers utilize a vertical design, featuring multiple stacked layers for efficient moisture removal, often suited for larger spaces. Rotor type dehumidifiers employ a rotating wheel system filled with desiccant material, facilitating continuous moisture absorption and condensation. Both types are effective in controlling humidity levels, but their applications differ based on space requirements, efficiency, and energy consumption, catering to diverse residential and commercial needs.</p></p>

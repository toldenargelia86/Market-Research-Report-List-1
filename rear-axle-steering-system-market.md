<p><p>The Rear Axle Steering System market is segmented into two primary types: Single/Central Actuator and Dual Actuator systems. </p><p>Single/Central Actuator systems utilize one actuator to control the rear wheels' steering angle, offering a simpler design and cost-effective solution. In contrast, Dual Actuator systems employ two separate actuators for enhanced performance and precision, allowing for more refined control of each wheel's movement. This differentiation caters to varying vehicle performance needs, influencing market dynamics and consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>

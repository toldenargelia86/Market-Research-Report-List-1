<p><strong>The Aircraft Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Seals Market comprises two primary types: Static and Dynamic. Static seals are used in non-moving parts to prevent leakage and maintain pressure, ensuring airtight and fluid-tight connections in components like fuel tanks and engine casings. Dynamic seals, on the other hand, are designed for moving parts, such as rotating shafts or actuators, to control fluid flow and reduce friction while maintaining performance under motion. Both are critical for aircraft safety, efficiency, and reliability, tailored to specific operational demands.</p></p>

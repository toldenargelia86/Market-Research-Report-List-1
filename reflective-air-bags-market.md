<p><p>The reflective air bags market encompasses two primary types: void fill and cushioning. Void fill air bags are designed to fill empty spaces in shipping packaging, preventing movement and damage during transit. These bags provide minimal resistance and are lightweight. In contrast, cushioning air bags are intended to absorb shock and protect fragile items by providing a softer landing. Both types utilize air-filled structures to offer efficient, protective solutions for various packaging needs, ensuring product safety on their journey.</p></p>

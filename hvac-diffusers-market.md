<p><p>The HVAC diffusers market consists of three primary types: ceiling-mounted, wall-mounted, and floor-mounted diffusers. Ceiling-mounted diffusers, commonly used in commercial spaces, distribute air evenly from above, enhancing ambient comfort. Wall-mounted diffusers, ideal for smaller areas, provide targeted airflow and are often integrated into the aesthetic of the room. Floor-mounted diffusers are typically installed in residential settings, allowing for effective air distribution closer to the floor, improving heating and cooling efficiency. Each type serves distinct architectural and functional needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The waterborne polyurethane adhesives market is segmented into three main types: anionic, cationic, and nonionic. Anionic adhesives, characterized by negatively charged groups, provide excellent adhesion and flexibility, ideal for various substrates. Cationic adhesives, with positively charged components, offer enhanced bonding on surfaces and good chemical resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications. Nonionic adhesives, being neutral, exhibit versatility and compatibility with a wide range of materials, focusing on environmental stability and user-friendliness in diverse applications.</p></p>

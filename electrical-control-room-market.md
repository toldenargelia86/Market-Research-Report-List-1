<p><p>The Electrical Control Room market is divided into two main types: Low Voltage Electrical Control Rooms and Medium Voltage Electrical Control Rooms. Low Voltage control rooms handle electrical systems operating at lower voltages, typically below 1 kV, focusing on applications in residential and commercial settings. In contrast, Medium Voltage control rooms manage systems operating between 1 kV and 35 kV, often used in industrial settings and substations. Both types are essential for managing and distributing power safely and efficiently within various infrastructures.</p></p>
